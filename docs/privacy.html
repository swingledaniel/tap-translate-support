Tap Translate

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

Overview

Tap Translate processes screenshots only when you choose to send a screenshot to the app, usually through an iOS Shortcut. The app is designed to recognize text in that screenshot, show selectable text boxes, and optionally translate or explain the recognized text.

Information Processed

The app may process screenshot images, recognized text, target language settings, translation settings, and selected text. Tap Translate does not sell personal information and does not use third-party advertising SDKs.

On-Device Processing

Text recognition and local translation options are processed on your device using system or on-device machine learning frameworks. These local features do not require Tap Translate to send your screenshot text to a developer-operated server.

Cloud Features

If you choose a cloud translation option or request an AI explanation, the recognized text from your screenshot may be sent to a cloud provider so the provider can return a translation or explanation. Tap Translate sends the text needed for the selected feature and does not intentionally send the full screenshot image for normal text translation or explanation features.

Cloud providers may process requests under their own terms and privacy policies. Current or planned providers include Google translation or Gemini services and Anthropic Claude services.

Storage

Tap Translate may store app settings, selected translation preferences, and credentials or tokens in the iOS Keychain. The app does not intentionally maintain a long-term history of your screenshots or recognized text. A production backend may keep limited operational logs for security, abuse prevention, rate limiting, and debugging.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, please use the support link on the Tap Translate support page.
